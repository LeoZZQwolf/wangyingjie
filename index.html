Personal Profile

Wang Yingjie, Postdoctoral Fellow, Member of the Communist Party of China. Currently Professor and Doctoral Supervisor at the School of Computer and Control Engineering, Yantai University, Deputy Director of the Office of Development Planning and Discipline Construction, Recipient of the Shandong Province Excellent Young Scientists Fund, Top-notch Talent of Yantai City Double Hundred Plan, Category B Talent of Yantai City, Director of the Shandong Provincial University Future Industry Engineering Research Center for Aerospace Information Fusion and Service Computing. IEEE Senior Member, CCF Distinguished Member, Publicity Chair and Diversity Chair of IEEE TCSVC, Standing Committee Member/Deputy Secretary-General of the Technical Committee on Service Computing of China Computer Federation (CCF), Standing Committee Member of the Technical Committee on Intelligent Service of China Association for Artificial Intelligence (CAAI), Executive Committee Member of the CCF Technical Committee on Artificial Intelligence and Pattern Recognition, Executive Committee Member of the CCF Technical Committee on Collaborative Computing, Corresponding Committee Member of the CAAI Technical Committee on Machine Learning, Member of the Young Professionals Group of the IoT Expert Committee of Chinese Institute of Electronics, Executive Director/Deputy Secretary-General/Secretary-General of the Standardization Working Committee of Shandong Artificial Intelligence Society, Executive Director of Shandong Computer Federation. Her main research interests include crowdsensing networks, service computing, and Artificial Intelligence. She has served as Principal Investigator for 14 research projects, including the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) Major Research Plan Cultivation Project/General Program/Youth Program, China Postdoctoral Science Foundation Special Grant/General Grant, Shandong Provincial Natural Science Foundation Excellent Young Scientists Program/General Program, and Yantai City Science and Technology Development Plan Project. She is the Leader of the Innovation Team under the Shandong Provincial University Youth Innovation Science and Technology Program. She has published 117 high-level academic papers in prestigious journals and international conferences such as IEEE TMC, IEEE TSC, IEEE TII, IEEE TCC, IEEE TCSS, IEEE Internet of Things Journal, Computer Networks, Journal of Software, IDEAL, WASA, and SocialCom, among which 77 papers are as first author or corresponding author, 5 papers are ESI Highly Cited Papers, 1 paper is a Hot Paper, and she has received 4 conference best paper awards. Her work has been cited more than 3,000 times in total, with 11 papers cited over 100 times each. She holds 20 authorized invention patents as the first inventor, including 1 US patent and 19 Chinese patents, among which 2 have been commercialized, and has participated in formulating 2 group standards. She has received numerous awards, including the First Prize of Shandong Province Science and Technology Progress Award (2025), the First Prize of Shandong Provincial Teaching Achievement Award (2025), the First Prize of Shandong Artificial Intelligence Natural Science Award (2023), CAAI Service Computing Young Talent Award (2022), Outstanding Youth Award of Shandong Artificial Intelligence Science and Technology Award (2020), the Third Prize of Shandong Natural Science Award (2021), and the Third Prize of Shandong Higher Education Science and Technology Award (2020). She has been honored with titles such as Model Woman Contributor of Shandong Province, Most Outstanding Female Striver of Yantai City, and Shandong Artificial Intelligence Society Most Beautiful Science and Technology Worker. She has served as Organizing Chair, Publicity Chair, Publication Chair, and other leadership roles for multiple international conferences, and serves as a reviewer for multiple prestigious journals. She also serves as a panel reviewer for the National Natural Science Foundation of China, Shandong Provincial Science and Technology Commissioner, and Corporate Technology Consultant.
