Personal Profile

Professor Yingjie Wang is a recipient of the Shandong Province Excellent Young Scientists Fund and a leading researcher in mobile crowdsensing, service computing, and AI. She has published extensively in top venues, holds multiple patents, and leads several major national and provincial projects.
